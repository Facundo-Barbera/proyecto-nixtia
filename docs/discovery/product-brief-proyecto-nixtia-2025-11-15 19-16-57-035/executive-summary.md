# Executive Summary

Nixtia is building a complete e-commerce platform for a premium corn-based food products business. The platform enables online sales through a customer-facing virtual store while providing business management tools via an admin dashboard. This MVP focuses on proving the complete customer journey (catalog → cart → checkout) and demonstrating business intelligence capabilities to win client confidence within a 2-3 day delivery window.

**Core Value:** Transform a WhatsApp/social media-based business into a professional digital commerce operation with self-service purchasing and real-time business analytics.

**Primary Challenge:** Ruthless scope management to deliver a demoable, polished MVP in 2-3 days that proves capability without overbuilding.

**Success Metric:** Client sees working virtual store + business dashboard and commits to full engagement.
