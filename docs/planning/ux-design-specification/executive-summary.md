# Executive Summary

Nixtia is an operational liberation platform for artisan food businesses trapped between manual WhatsApp chaos and expensive e-commerce platforms. The UX design prioritizes **elder-friendly**, **frictionless checkout** that feels familiar to WhatsApp users while providing the business owner with professional analytics and control.

**Core Experience Goals:**

- **Customers:** Browse → Add to Cart → Checkout in under 60 seconds on mobile
- **Admin:** Log in → See business insights instantly → Feel empowered

**Target Emotional Response:**

- Customers: "This is easier than WhatsApp!"
- Admin: "I finally _understand_ my business."
